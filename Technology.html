The Best Soil Erosion Polymer Sheet Pile in the Market

Our “Dingcheng” brand is the best value polymer composite plastic steel sheet pile. This system has been proven to be very effective when used as flood control walls. It is the most economical and effective solution for protecting coastal land & vulnerable flooding areas from erosion.

It is long-lasting, much cheaper than steel sheet piles & concrete substitutes, resistant to UV, will not rust, corrode or peel. The construction is simple and can be rapidly installed with minimal fuss & ground preparation, using just a backhoe loader or excavator, installed with a vibe hammer, plate compactor or compression driver.

The retaining walls built with synthetic sheet piles will last for years and is a permanent solution to protect soil erosion. It is an attractive & functional product and will add value to any development requiring water control.

Modular House & Buildings

We are pleased to introduce the HEGE brand of Prefabricated Houses into the Bangladesh market. The product is of high quality, price sensitive & manufactured to ISO9001 International System Certification. 
This Modular House trend is a new type of building structure that is catching on world-wide due to the short lead-time required to put them up. One of the most innovative and class leading products is the HEGE Folding Container House. It is movable, wind/shock proof, corrosion resistant, fully insulated, requires minimal or no foundation on installation site, low cost and can be supplied in a range from low to high-end finishes.








HEGE Folding Container House is perfect for a wide range of applications: project manager’s office, mobile office, staff/army dormitory and military/emergency command centre. This is the lower cost model which offers flexibility & speed. It can be re-folded & transported to another location multiple times 
The installation & assembly is quick and easy, requiring only 2-3 people about 3 hours to complete. It is also easy to transport by sea or land. They can be easily loaded into a 40’ container for sea shipment and 14 units can be loaded onto a 17.5 platform trailer truck for transportation on land to save big on transportation costs.









HEGE Flat-Pack Container House is the prefabricated modular folding model where all facets are fully customisable; customers can specify the walls, the doors & number of windows, as well as expanding the structure sideways and stackable to 3 units high. This option offers great flexibility to build small offices with meeting rooms & reception areas, base & modular camps, quarantine centres, resort villas and multi-storey flat accommodations with complete facilities. Please have a look at the options, we think you will be very pleased

Railway Sleepers

We are proud to bring you the complete line of prestressed concrete sleepers for the railway industry which are manufactured by Zhejiang Longyou Woxin Railway Equipment Co. Ltd. 
This company is the first private enterprise that specialises in this manufacturing, started production in 2005 and presently has 10 production lines producing more than 150 types of railway sleepers. The company has passed ISO9001 Quality Management System Certification and has recently developed in collaboration with the Southwest Jiaotong University a new Type III Elastic Bridge Sleeper which helps to dampen sound & vibration for railway overpasses and won acclamation by the Ministry of Railways. 
In 2013, the company became an export base as its products gained valuable export markets in Australia & Europe and continues to innovate for its good quality and after-sales service.
